frequently asked

How do I create a reel?
Tap the plus icon, select 'Reel', and upload your vertical video. Add effects and music.
How to grow followers?
Post consistently, use trending hashtags, and engage with your community daily.
What video format works best?
Vertical 9:16 ratio, MP4 format, under 60 seconds for maximum reach.
Can I monetize my content?
Yes! Once you hit 10k followers, you can join our creator program.
How to add copyrighted music?
Use our licensed music library to avoid copyright issues.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all messages and posts.
How long do stories last?
Stories disappear after 24 hours, but you can save them to highlights.
Can I have multiple accounts?
Yes! You can add up to 5 accounts and switch easily.
How to block someone?
Go to their profile, tap three dots, and select 'Block'.
Can I download my data?
Request your data in settings > privacy > download data.
